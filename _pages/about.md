I am Santiago Llorens, a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Gdańsk in the group of [Felix Huber](https://huberfe.github.io). Previously, I was a Ph.D. student at [Grup d'Informació i Fenòmens Quàntics](https://webs.uab.cat/giq/) (Autonomous University of Barcelona) under the supervision of Prof. [Ramon Muñoz Tapia](https://scholar.google.es/citations?user=SGxT9bkAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao) and Dr. [Gael Sentís Herrera](https://scholar.google.es/citations?user=ai_4KiEAAAAJ&hl=en). Before that, I completed a double degree in physics and chemistry at the Autonomous University of Barcelona and a master in quantum science and technology at the Basque Country University, Bilbao, under the supervision of Prof. [Jens Siewert](https://scholar.google.es/citations?hl=en&user=ryYiAcwAAAAJ).

## Research interests

My research is strongly focused on applying mathematical tools to quantum information theory problems. My main interests inside the field include:

* **Graph theory applied to quantum information:** This is my current focus as a postdoc. I am using my mathematical background to study quantum error-correcting codes, AME states, and quantum information tasks closely related to graphs.
* **Quantum state and channel discrimination:** During my Ph.D., I specialized in these tasks using semidefinite programming (SDP), algebraic combinatorics, and group and representation theory.
* **Bloch representation of quantum states:** I have been working on this topic since my master's and in subsequent collaborations, with a particular focus on correlated systems.
